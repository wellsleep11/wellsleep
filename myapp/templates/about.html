SPRING MATTRESS

A spring mattress is a type of bed that contains springs or coils within its core , providing support and comfort for the sleeper. It typically has a cushioned top layer and is available in a range of firmness levels.

BEDSPREAD

A bedspread is a decorative covering that drapes over a mattress and reaches the floor. It's used to add style to a bedroom and can be made from a variety of materials, such as cotton, linen, or polyester.

WHAT WE DO

MATTRESS

We use a mattress for sleeping and resting, choosing one that suits our preferences and needs, regularly cleaning and maintaining it to prolong its lifespan and ensure hygiene, and eventually replacing it when it no longer provides adequate support and comfort.

CUSHIONS

Cushions are used for comfort and decoration, placed on sofas, chairs, or beds. We choose ones that match our decor and preferences, fluff them regularly to maintain their shape, and clean them periodically to ensure hygiene.

DECORATION

Decoration is used to enhance the aesthetic appeal of a space. We choose decorative items that suit our tastes and style, arrange them creatively, and maintain them periodically to keep the space looking fresh and inviting.

CURTAINS

Curtains are used to provide privacy, regulate natural light, and add style to a room. We choose ones that match our decor and preferences, regularly clean and maintain them, and eventually replace them when they show signs of wear and tear.

BED COVER

Bedcovers are used to protect bedding and add style to a bedroom. We choose ones that match our decor and preferences, regularly wash and maintain them, and replace them periodically to keep the bed looking fresh and clean.

BLANKETS

Blankets are cozy and versatile, keeping you warm while sleeping or relaxing on the couch. Available in different fabrics, sizes, and styles, they are a must-have for anyone looking to stay comfortable and snug.
